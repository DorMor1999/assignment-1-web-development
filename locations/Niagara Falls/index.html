Niagara Falls is a world-famous waterfall located on the border between the United States and Canada. It consists of three separate waterfalls: the Horseshoe Falls, the American Falls, and the Bridal Veil Falls. Known for its stunning beauty and immense power, Niagara Falls is a popular tourist destination, drawing millions of visitors each year. It offers various attractions and activities, including boat tours, observation decks, and scenic viewpoints, allowing visitors to experience the awe-inspiring force of nature up close.
